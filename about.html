About HWorks

Mission Statement

HWorks is an initiative to encourage the building and promotion of digital tools to help modern students stay organized and excel in school.

Who's behind all this?

The HWorks initiative was started in Janurary 2013 by Pennsylvania high school student and software developer Avi Romanoff. Before launching HWorks, he was an active core contributor and council member of elementary, an open-source project which is building a next-generation open desktop platform with a focus on user experience and design. He also created and maintains Keeba, the first project to be part of the HWorks Initiative and the impetus for the creation of HWorks itself.

This Website

The HWorks website itself (what you're looking at right now) is completely open-source and available for others to contribute to. Pull requests and bug reports are welcome at the project's official GitHub page.

Contact

You can get in touch with us via email at howdy@hworks.org, or by joining and/or posting to the official Google Group (mailing list) here. We also hold monthly Skype meetings the first Saturday of every month. Watch the Google Group for details!
