    • Homework ≠ Headache.

    Here are core values necessary for digital homework management:

    One source of truth.

    Homework comes at kids from all directions. Orally in class, class wikis, and as class hand-outs. Any digital approach to homework management should seek to minimize redundancy and conflict.

    Seamlessly integrated.

    Homework should work with the teachers and school assigning it -- not against them. Minimize re-training costs by plugging into existing systems and routines where possible and practical.

    Naturally prioritized.

    Real-life schedules are never as simplistic as we expect them to be.

    Homework management should focus on building schedules that promote effective time management, while still following deadlines.

    Intuitive & streamlined.

    Managing your academic life using digital tools shouldn't require a manual or a tutorial.

    Aim to be as simple as a pencil and paper to-do list.
