Keeba

Visit | GitHub

Started as the personal project of a high-school sophomore, Keeba has grown to become a fully-featured homework management web app that has been adopted and sponsored by his school.

By integrating with the school's existing homework portal (through a web-scraping backend), Keeba enables students to interact with their homework directly. Rather than a simple listing of teacher bulletins, students can mark assignments as done, move around personal due dates to suit their schedule, and track their own courses and tasks which are not part of the school's official website. This functionality is exposed through an intuitive, modern and stream-lined interface using modern web technology to keep students in-sync and on top of their work wherever they are.
