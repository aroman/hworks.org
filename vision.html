Homework ≠ Headache

These are core values necessary for digital homework management:

One source of truth

Homework comes from all directions: orally in class, course wikis, and hand-outs. A digital approach to homework management should minimize redundancy and conflict.

Seamlessly integrated

Teachers should be able to assign homework using their existing systems. The underlying digital homework management infrastructure should be the bridge between clunky school software and the student's individualized homework portal.

Naturally prioritized

Students' real-life schedules are never as simple as they expect them to be. Homework management should build schedules that promote effective use of time.

Intuitive & streamlined

Use of digital tools shouldn't require a manual or a complicated tutorial. The digital process should be as simple as pencil and paper.
